Angler

XXX - AEK - Axpergle - Status: Retired - Last seen: 2016-06-07

The Angler Exploit Kit has been the most popular and evolved exploit kit from 2014 to middle of 2016. There was several variations. The historical "indexm" variant was used to spread Lurk. A vip version used notabily to spread Poweliks, the "standard" commercial version, and a declinaison tied to load selling (mostly bankers) that can be associated to EmpirePPC
